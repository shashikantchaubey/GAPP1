Home Number Sum Game

Rules

  • Candidates must check which numbers are falling and collect those which sum to the target number.
  • Be careful to avoid the other falling numbers to avoid Penalty
  • Aspects: quantitative reasoning, perceptual speed, memory span.
  • 4 Seconds will be allotted to remember the numbers on falling balls.
  • There will be Six rounds for this particular section
  • 1 point will be deducted as a plenty
